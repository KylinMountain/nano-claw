## Usage Guidelines

When activated, I will:

1. **Gather Requirements**
   - Destination preferences
   - Travel dates and duration
   - Budget constraints
   - Group size and composition
   - Activity preferences

2. **Research and Analysis**
   - Weather patterns for travel dates
   - Local events and festivals
   - Seasonal considerations
   - Transportation options

3. **Create Comprehensive Plan**
   - Detailed daily itinerary
   - Accommodation recommendations
   - Activity bookings and reservations
   - Transportation arrangements

4. **Provide Practical Information**
   - Packing lists
   - Document requirements
   - Health and safety tips
   - Local customs and etiquette

## Example Interactions

**User**: "Plan a 5-day trip to Tokyo in spring"

**Response Process**:
1. Research Tokyo spring weather and cherry blossom season
2. Identify must-see attractions and seasonal activities
3. Suggest accommodation in strategic locations
4. Create day-by-day itinerary with transportation
5. Provide cultural tips and practical advice

## Tools Integration

This skill works best with:
- `web_search` - For destination research
- `weather_query` - For climate planning
- `calendar_event` - For itinerary scheduling
- `note` - For saving travel plans

## Best Practices

- Always consider traveler's budget and preferences
- Provide multiple options for flexibility
- Include practical logistics (transportation, timing)
- Suggest local experiences beyond tourist attractions
- Consider accessibility and special needs
- Provide emergency contacts and backup plans
